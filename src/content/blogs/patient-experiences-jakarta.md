description:
  - "Our analysis unveils both positive aspects and areas for improvement in hospital services, with 65% of reviews expressing positive sentiments. Notably, pharmacy services received the most negative feedback, while medical team collaboration garnered widespread appreciation."
keyFindings:
  - "Hospitals like RS Pondok Indah - Pondok Indah and RS Pondok Indah - Puri Indah boast high ratings and popularity, while RSUP Persahabatan and RSUP Fatmawati received lower ratings."
  - "Sentiment analysis revealed that 65% of reviews had a positive tone, indicating a willingness to provide feedback and appreciation for hospital services."
  - "Ten distinct topics emerged from the reviews, ranging from parking convenience and medical services to pharmacy processes and patient care."
  - "Pharmacy services received the most negative sentiment (70%), with complaints mainly revolving around wait times and queues. Conversely, medical team collaboration received predominantly positive sentiment, highlighting patient appreciation for vaccination services and specialist medical care."

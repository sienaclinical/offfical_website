description:
  - "It has been reported that HPV screening coverage among Indonesian women is very low, leading to advanced diagnosis of cervical cancer. As part of our series of events and studies on HPV and cervical pre-cancer, we conducted a webinar to educate the public and healthcare workers about the importance of HPV screening. During the session, we also shared the disease progression of cervical cancer so participants could better understand the critical importance of early screening."
  - "This webinar was conducted in collaboration with our research partners:"
  - "1. Genepica - London, UK"
  - "2. Summit Institute for Development - Lombok, Indonesia"
  - "3. Bio Farma - Bandung, Indonesia"

description: "Prostate cancer ranks as the sixth most common cancer among men in Indonesia. The exact causes of prostate cancer are not fully understood, but several factors contribute to its development, including genetic, lifestyle, environmental influences, and lack of awareness and screening. Notably, prostate cancer survival rates in SEA, including Indonesia, are generally lower than those in Europe and North America. This disparity may be attributed to less access to diagnostic facilities and higher age at disease recognition compared to advanced countries. Improving early detection and access to quality healthcare services is crucial for enhancing survival outcomes for prostate cancer patients in Indonesia, particularly in low-resource settings."
